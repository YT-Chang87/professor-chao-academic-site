Prof. How-Ran Chao conducts interdisciplinary research on air pollutants, nanomaterial toxicity, environmental exposure and health risk assessment, persistent organic pollutants, microbial indicators, and environmental monitoring. His recent work addresses PM2.5 toxicity, flame-retardant exposure, child and neonatal health indicators, climate change, and greenhouse-gas reduction.
